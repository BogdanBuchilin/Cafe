Our Story

Founded in 2010, Brew Haven started as a small coffee cart in Vancouver's Stanley Park. What began as a passion project quickly grew into one of the city's most beloved coffee destinations.

We dedicate ourselves to ethical sourcing, working directly with coffee farmers in Colombia, Ethiopia, and Guatemala. Our signature roasting process, developed over a decade, brings out the unique flavors of each bean.

Now located in Gastown's historic district, we continue to serve exceptional coffee while fostering community connections.
